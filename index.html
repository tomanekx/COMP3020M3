Welcome to TinderEats

Are you ready to find the perfect meal today? TinderEats uses matchmaking algorithms to find the perfect restaraunt based on what you like. How it all works is by swiping right to let us know that you like an item, and swiping left to dislike an item. If you happen to see something that really catches your eye you can also add it directly to your cart and check out the rest of the that restaurant. You can also skip this process and just directly start looking for restaurants in the matches section.
